## Minds that burn brightly

A psyker possesses an unusually strong connection to the Immaterium. Through concentration, instinct, ritual, or training, that mind can draw Warp energy into realspace. The result may be telepathy, precognition, telekinesis, destructive force, healing, or stranger effects.

Power does not guarantee control. Every act of psychic exertion makes the psyker more visible to the Warp. A careless mind can attract entities, suffer catastrophic backlash, or become the opening through which daemons enter reality.

## Humanity's dangerous evolution

Humanity is becoming a more psychic species, but its institutions are shaped by fear. The Imperium identifies psykers through the Black Ships of the Adeptus Astra Telepathica. Some are trained as sanctioned psykers or Astropaths. Some are consumed to sustain vital Imperial systems. Many are judged too unstable to live.

This cruelty is not presented as clean necessity. It is one of the setting's defining moral horrors: real danger exists, and the Imperium answers it with industrialized terror.

<Callout title="Sanctioned does not mean safe">
Imperial sanction means a psyker has survived selection, testing, and binding to official service. It does not remove the Warp's risks or the suspicion of ordinary citizens.
</Callout>

## Species and souls

Different species register differently in the Warp. Aeldari souls burn with great intensity and remain vulnerable to Slaanesh after death. Orks generate a collective psychic field that reinforces their culture and technology. Tyranids project the Shadow in the Warp, a vast psychic pressure caused by the Hive Mind. Necrons possess no living souls in the ordinary sense, the result of their ancient biotransference.

## Faith, sorcery, and uncertainty

The border between psychic power, faith, and sorcery is deliberately unstable. Miracles associated with Imperial belief can produce material effects. Chaos rituals use symbolism, sacrifice, and knowledge to shape the Warp. The setting rarely offers a comfortable separation between sincere devotion and psychic mechanism.

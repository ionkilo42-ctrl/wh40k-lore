## The Great Game

The four great Chaos Gods are patterns of emotion and desire grown into immense Warp intelligences. They war against one another in the endless **Great Game**, even while their servants attack realspace.

- **Khorne** embodies rage, violence, martial pride, and slaughter.
- **Tzeentch** embodies ambition, change, knowledge, and manipulation.
- **Nurgle** embodies despair, endurance, decay, and the comfort of surrender.
- **Slaanesh** embodies excess, obsession, perfection, and insatiable appetite.

Their domains overlap because mortal emotions do. A warrior's honorable fury can feed Khorne. A healer's desperate hope can attract Tzeentch. Chaos corrupts by turning understandable desires into absolute demands.

## Gifts and chains

Chaos promises agency in a brutal galaxy. Its followers may receive strength, knowledge, resilience, or sensation beyond mortal limits. Those gifts reshape the recipient until the original motive no longer matters. The servant becomes a clearer expression of the patron's hunger.
